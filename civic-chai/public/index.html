How Civic Chai Works

1

Citizen Reports a Problem

Scan QR code or open the website. Choose issue type, add photo (optional), and submit in under 30 seconds. No essays, no long forms.

Quick Scan Photo Upload Under 30 Seconds
2

System Decides Criticality

Civic Chai automatically analyzes: issue type, location, evidence, and history. Then it assigns a priority level that doesn't exist in current platforms.

Critical High Medium Low
3

Time-Bound Action is Enforced

Each priority level has a deadline. If ignored, it auto-escalates to senior officials. Citizens see this escalation in real-time.

SLA Tracking Auto-Escalation Transparency
4

Authority Must Show Proof

To close an issue, authorities upload after-photos and describe action taken. Citizens can accept or reject‚Äîif rejected, the issue reopens.

Photo Evidence Citizen Verification Reopen if Needed
5

Performance Becomes Visible

Instead of star ratings, Civic Chai shows average fix time, SLA compliance, and repeated failures. This creates accountability without shaming.

Fix Time Metrics Compliance Tracking Data-Driven
